## 🎯 2. Executive Summary
### Core Objective
The Space Agent aims to bring the wonders of space and astronomy directly to users. It provides immediate, accurate answers to astronomy-related questions, displays NASA's daily scientific imagery (Astronomy Picture of the Day - APOD) complete with details and media, and lets users build a personal gallery of their favorite space pictures.

### High-Level Success Criteria
*   Users can ask natural language questions about astronomy and get accurate, informative answers.
*   Users can retrieve NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day for any valid date.
*   Users can save specific pictures to a personal favorites database and view them later via a dedicated widget.
*   The agent handles conversational fallbacks gracefully on SMS and Voice channels.
